Fall

By Samantha Sullivan, 11/28/16

This is a summary of the clothing sister missionaries for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints should have to wear during the Fall season in Toronto, Canada.

Shoes

Suggested footwear in the Fall season ranges from regular summer footwear like flats and close-toed sandals to boots.

Skirts/Dresses

Skirts and dresses in early fall should be knee length and loose. As the season progresses, they should get to be mid-calf length but no longer than that.

Shirts/Jackets

Shirts should be short-sleeved, but can be longer sleeved as needed as the temperature changes. Jackets should be light in color and fabric for comfort, but can be heavier.

Accessories

Gloves are suggested for the end of the fall season.
