Purpose Statement

I am building this site to provide consumers with easy access to the day’s weather so they can plan their day accordingly and won’t get caught unawares of what the weather will bring.


Audience Description

The Audience is 19-25 years old, accessed through mobile device, not much money, lives frugally, they are accessing the site because they want to know how to dress for class or for a group activity they are doing.


Persona

Name: Mariah Taylor

Age: 21

Income: Less than $10,000 a year

Hobbies: Having fun with friends, being outside, anything but homework.

Career: Mariah wants to be business manager when she graduates, and own her own business. She thinks running a cupcake store would be pretty fun.

Goals: Mariah want’s to check the weather easily before she leave her apartment for class or goes outside for an activity. She wants to be able to look at it quickly so she can make a decision quickly.

Quote: “Wow, it looks cold outside. I wonder how cold it really is out there?”
